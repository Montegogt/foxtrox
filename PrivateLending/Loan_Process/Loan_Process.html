Apply
The loan application is the 2nd step on purpose. We won’t ask you to apply if we haven’t already talked and are confident that this loan is right for you. We do not require bank statements, tax returns, debt to income ratios or minimum FICO scores to make our decisions and have created a simple online application that takes less than 10 minutes to complete.

3

Get Funded
Once we have your loan application we open or engage with escrow. It typically takes 2-3 business days to receive a title report and one day for us to order loan docs. Clients can sign at escrow or with a mobile notary and we close and fund the same day. It's as simple as that!

4

Pay Your Bill
We make it easy to pay your bill and track your loan through our on-line portal. While most clients choose to use their own on-line banking to make their payments, you can always mail us a check or money order and unlike most lenders we do accept CASH or Zelle payments. We also enable our clients to set up recurring ACH payments through our simple online portal.
