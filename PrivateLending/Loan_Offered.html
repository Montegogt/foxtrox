1031 & Reverse 1031 Exchange Loans

In simple terms, a 1031 or reverse 1031 exchange is the way expert real estate investors defer taxes on profits made from the sale of one or several investment properties through the reinvestment of those profits into another investment property. The tax savings can be a huge, but it does require proper documentation and is very time sensitive! If you miss the government’s timelines you will not qualify for the tax benefit. NW Private Lending can connect you with a local 1031 exchange expert and can close on purchases within 3-5 business days. We specialize in last-minute solutions to complex transactions.
