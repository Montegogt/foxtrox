THE "BAJA CLAW"™ BY M/T®

Mickey Thompson® Performance Tires and Wheels is proud to unleash the all new extreme terrain tire, the"Baja Claw"™. This tire is available in a 33x13.50-15LT size. Sizes to follow are: 33x13.50-16LT, 35x13.50-15LT, 35x13.50-16LT, 31x11.50-15LT and 31x11.50-16LT. This new tire is a polyester/fiberglass bias belted construction.

The M/T® Baja Claw features 20" of contact patch from sidebiter® to sidebiter®, (20" of surface area on 13.50" tread width-designated tires) and a 6-ply cut resistant sidewall compound for even more stability and strength. These tires also feature circumferential grooves to allow the carcass to flex and conform to rocky terrain during low air pressure, slow moving and rock crawling. The 6-ply Baja Claw™ has a "C" load range as well.

The all new M/T® tread pattern and extra long sidebiter® design also allows you to dig out of any terrain you find yourself in. The "Monster Truck" type tread is a directional, long wearing and self-cleaning design. Even with the aggressive tread design, the Baja Claw™ has a relatively low noise level on the highway.
