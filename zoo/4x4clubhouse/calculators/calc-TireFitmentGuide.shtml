Tire Fitment Guide

   

Tire Fitment Guide for Lifted Applications 

This chart is designed to give you an idea of approximately what tire can fit you vehicle after installing a lift kit. This chart will also let you know what type of typical modifications may be necessary to accommodate your selection of tires. This chart is intended as a reference guide and is not absolute. It is based on standard width tires, wheels and off-set. Install your kit and then verify wheel/tire fitment before installing them. 

Please NOTE: When changing your tire size it will have a direct effect on power and drivability. To make sure that you make the best choice possible check out our Gear Ratio Guide for Larger Tires.
