GEAR RATIO CALCULATOR

This calc can be used to find any missing value.

If your tires are bigger than stock you probably, like myself, have spend a lot of time trying to WAG (wild ass guess) my actual MPH when going down the road. If you know your gear ratios and tire size you can get a fairly close idea here. Fill in all entries except the one you want an answer to (in green section). If you'd like to find your overall crawl ratio, enter the Diff/Trans/TransfCase ratios. Remember, the yellow section (crawl ratio) is only for information and doesn't require you to erase or enter anything there.
